1. Self-Assessment

  • Identify your interests, strengths, weaknesses, and personality.
  • Use aptitude/interest tests for guidance.

2. Explore Options

  • Research different streams, entrance exams, job roles, and future demand.
  • Talk to mentors, teachers, and professionals.

3. Set Goals

  • Short-term: Board exams, entrance preparation.
  • Long-term: Decide career/job role you wish to pursue.

4. Create a Roadmap

  • Choose the right subjects & entrance exams.
  • Plan higher studies, professional courses, or vocational training.

5. Skill Development

  • Learn coding, designing, communication, public speaking.
  • Join internships, workshops, volunteering programs.

6. Stay Flexible

  • Careers evolve with technology and economy.
  • Be open to new opportunities and changes.

7. Seek Guidance

  • Take help from parents, teachers, career counselors.
  • Use guidance platforms, seminars, mentorship programs.
